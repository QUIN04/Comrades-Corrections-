London

London is the capital city of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom,with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

Standing on the River Thames, London has been a major settlement for two millennia, its history going back to its founding by the Romans, who named it Londonium.

DOUALA

Douala is the chief metropolis of Cameroon.It has a population of over five million people.The seaport and airport make Douala the main entry and exit town for people and goods.

As a Regional and Divisional heaquarters, The Governor of the Littoral and the SDO of Wouri reside here.
